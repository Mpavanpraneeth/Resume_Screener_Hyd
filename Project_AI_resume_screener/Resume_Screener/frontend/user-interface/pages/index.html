Why Resume AI?

Upload and scan resumes for ATS compatibility

Resume AI checks whether your resume can pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), which many companies use to filter candidates. It analyzes formatting, structure, and keyword usage to ensure your resume reaches human recruiters.

Match keywords from job descriptions

It compares your resume against specific job postings to identify missing or mismatched keywords. This helps tailor your resume to each role, improving your chances of getting noticed.

Track your resume improvement over time

Resume AI stores past versions and scores, allowing you to monitor changes in ATS compatibility, keyword matches, and layout quality. This helps refine your resume strategy over time.

Get AI-powered feedback and layout tips

It provides smart suggestions on wording, formatting, and design—such as font choices, section order, and readability—to make your resume more professional and visually appealing.

How It Works

Resume AI analyzes your resume using advanced algorithms to assess formatting, keyword relevance, and technical depth. It then provides actionable feedback and scoring to help you improve.
